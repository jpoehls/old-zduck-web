FILE 13

ZWord is a project I started to create a web based Bible Study application. Its claim to fame would be a minimalist design focused on usability and the ability for users to highlight scriptures and enter personal study notes that are stored online and could be optionally shared with others.

This project has been on hold for several years and I'm not sure if it'll get picked back up again or not.
